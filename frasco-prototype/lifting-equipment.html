A Wide Variety Of Solutions
For Every Situation

Lifting equipment failures are among the most dangerous and costly incidents in industrial facilities. Frasco provides comprehensive certification and inspection programs for all types of lifting equipment — ensuring regulatory compliance and the safety of your workforce.

From wire rope assessments to full crane certifications, our inspectors evaluate equipment to applicable provincial regulations and CSA standards, delivering the documentation you need to operate with confidence.

Why Certify?

Uncertified or out-of-date lifting equipment puts workers at risk and exposes your operation to regulatory penalties and liability. Frasco keeps your certification current and your lifts safe.

Scope

Six Lifting Equipment
Categories

01

Certification

Comprehensive lifting equipment certification programs covering initial certification, periodic inspection, and re-certification to all applicable provincial and national regulations and CSA standards.

02

Wire Rope

Electromagnetic and visual inspection of wire rope for broken wires, corrosion, loss of metallic area, kinks, and fatigue. Essential for crane, hoist, and rigging safety compliance.

03

Fork Lifts

Annual forklift certification and inspection covering mast assembly, forks, hydraulics, brakes, load capacity, and safety devices. Documentation for WorkSafe and regulatory bodies.

04

Gantry Cranes

Full structural, mechanical, and electrical inspection of free-standing and rail-mounted gantry cranes. Load test witnessing, runway alignment, and end-stop verification.

05

Cranes & Components

Overhead bridge cranes, jib cranes, hoists, hooks, slings, and rigging hardware. NDE of critical structural weldments and hooks, load testing, and re-certification packages.

06

Work Station Cranes

Under-hung and top-running work station crane systems including monorails, enclosed track, and KBK systems. Certification, structural assessment, and periodic re-inspection.

Safety First

Crane Inspection That
Keeps Workers Safe

Overhead cranes are among the most hazardous equipment in industrial facilities. A missed crack in a hook, a worn wire rope, or a failed limit switch can be catastrophic. Frasco's certified inspection program removes the guesswork.

  • NDE of critical weldments and hooks
  • Wire rope electromagnetic inspection (LEMC)
  • Load test witnessing and documentation
  • Complete certification package for regulators
  • Scheduled re-inspection reminders
