Know What's Inside Your Tubes Before It Fails

Frasco provides complete tube bundle inspection using eddy current and near-field testing — the fastest, most accurate non-destructive methods available for heat-transfer equipment. Our portable systems come directly to your facility.

From chillers to fin fans, we evaluate tube wall condition across seven equipment types and deliver a comprehensive report on-site — enabling data-driven maintenance planning without unnecessary shutdowns.

Why Tube Inspection?

A single failed tube can shut down an entire heat exchanger. Early detection of wall loss, pitting, and stress cracking prevents unplanned downtime and costly emergency repairs.

Scope of Inspection

Seven Equipment
Types Covered

01

Chillers

HVAC and industrial chiller tube bundle inspection using multi-frequency eddy current. Detect corrosion, erosion, and pitting across copper and titanium tubes.

02

Condensers

Steam and refrigeration condenser inspection for power and process facilities. Identifies plugged, thinned, and cracked tubes before leakage occurs.

03

Evaporators

Refrigeration and industrial evaporator coil and tube inspection. Detects fouling, deposits and wall thickness reduction to keep energy efficiency high.

04

Coolers

Lube oil, intercooler, aftercooler, and process cooler tube inspection. Prevents cross-contamination and unexpected thermal equipment failure.

05

Heat Exchangers

Shell-and-tube heat exchanger inspection across refining, chemical and petrochemical processes. Multi-frequency ECT, NFT and IRIS available.

06

Fin Fans

Air-cooled heat exchanger (fin fan) tube and header inspection. Near-field testing handles finned and fouled external surfaces without removal.

07

Boilers

Fire-tube and water-tube boiler inspection. Detects overheating, internal and external corrosion, pitting and deposition — critical for safe, compliant boiler operation.

Primary Technology

Eddy Current Tube Testing (ECT)

ECT is the fastest, most versatile method for non-ferrous tube inspection. Frasco's systems deliver high-speed automated scanning with data acquisition, producing colour-coded tube maps of the entire bundle.

  • Copper, titanium, brass, stainless, Admiralty
  • Detect OD and ID defects simultaneously
  • Colour-coded tube condition map provided
  • Full bundle inspection in hours, not days
  • Near Field Testing (NFT) for ferromagnetic tubes
